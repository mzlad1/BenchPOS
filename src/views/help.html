Quick Start Guide

Last updated: March 28, 2025

This guide will help you quickly set up and start using MZLAD Billing System for your business operations.

Installation

MZLAD Billing System is a desktop application that requires installation on your computer:

  1. Download the installer

    Visit our website and download the appropriate installer for your operating system (Windows, macOS, or Linux).

  2. Run the installer

    Double-click the downloaded file and follow the on-screen instructions.

  3. Complete the installation

    Choose your installation location, create shortcuts, and complete the installation process.

  4. Launch the application

    Use the desktop shortcut or start menu entry to launch MZLAD Billing System.

Note: You may need administrator privileges to install the application on your computer.

First Login

When you launch MZLAD Billing System for the first time, you'll need to create an admin account:

  1. Create admin account

    Enter your name, email, and create a secure password for the administrator account.

  2. Verify your email

    Check your email for a verification link and click it to verify your account.

  3. Login with admin credentials

    Use your email and password to log into the system.

Login screen of MZLAD Billing System

Business Setup

Configure your business information in the Settings section:

  1. Navigate to Settings

    Click on the "Settings" icon in the sidebar menu.

  2. Go to Business Information

    Select the "Business Info" tab from the settings sidebar.

  3. Enter business details

    Enter your business name, address, contact information, and tax ID.

  4. Upload your logo

    Add your business logo which will appear on invoices and receipts.

  5. Set currency and tax settings

    Configure your default currency and tax rates.

  6. Save changes

    Click the "Save Changes" button to apply your business settings.

Adding Products

Start building your inventory by adding products:

  1. Go to Inventory

    Click on the "Inventory" icon in the sidebar menu.

  2. Add a new product

    Click the "Add Product" button to open the product form.

  3. Enter product details

    Add the product name, SKU, category, price, cost, and initial stock quantity.

  4. Save the product

    Click "Save" to add the product to your inventory.

üí°
Pro Tip: You can also import multiple products at once using the "Import Products" feature with a CSV file.

Creating Sales

Process sales and generate invoices:

  1. Go to New Sale

    Click on the "New Sale" icon in the sidebar menu.

  2. Add items to cart

    Search for products and click on them to add to the cart, or scan barcodes if available.

  3. Adjust quantities

    Change the quantity of items as needed.

  4. Add customer information

    Enter customer details if desired for the receipt.

  5. Apply discounts if needed

    Add any applicable discounts to individual items or the entire sale.

  6. Complete the sale

    Click "Complete Sale" button to process the transaction.

  7. Print or email receipt

    Generate a receipt that can be printed or emailed to the customer.

System Requirements

Last updated: March 20, 2025

MZLAD Billing System is designed to run efficiently on modern computers. Check if your system meets the requirements below.

Minimum Requirements

Component Minimum Requirement Recommended
Operating System
  • Windows 10 (64-bit)
  • macOS 11 (Big Sur) or later
  • Ubuntu 20.04 LTS or later
  • Windows 11 (64-bit)
  • macOS 12 (Monterey) or later
  • Ubuntu 22.04 LTS or later
Processor Intel Core i3 or equivalent Intel Core i5/i7 or equivalent
Memory (RAM) 4 GB 8 GB or more
Disk Space 500 MB free space 1 GB or more free space
Display 1366 x 768 resolution 1920 x 1080 resolution or higher
Internet Connection Required for initial setup and synchronization Broadband connection for optimal performance

Additional Requirements for Specific Features

Receipt Printing

  • Thermal receipt printer (compatible with ESC/POS commands)
  • Standard desktop printer for A4/Letter paper receipts

Barcode Scanning

  • USB Barcode Scanner compatible with your operating system
  • Webcam with at least 720p resolution for QR code scanning

Multi-Device Sync

  • Internet connection on all devices
  • Firebase account (free tier is sufficient for small businesses)

Data Backup

  • External storage device or cloud storage account
  • Sufficient storage space for regular backups

Supported Browsers (for Web Sync Portal)

If you use the optional web sync portal, it's supported on the following browsers:

  • Google Chrome (latest 2 versions)
  • Mozilla Firefox (latest 2 versions)
  • Microsoft Edge (latest 2 versions)
  • Safari (latest 2 versions)

Important: While MZLAD Billing System can operate offline, an internet connection is required for initial setup, updates, and data synchronization between devices.

Performance Considerations

The performance of MZLAD Billing System may vary based on:

  • The size of your product database (5,000+ products may require more RAM)
  • The number of concurrent users if multiple terminals are in use
  • The frequency of synchronization between devices
  • The size and number of images stored for products

For large businesses with 10,000+ products or high transaction volumes, we recommend upgrading to the recommended specifications for optimal performance.
